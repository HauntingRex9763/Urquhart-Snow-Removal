About Us

Welcome to Urquhart's Snow Removal Ltd.! We are a family-owned business that has been providing snow removal services to the Moncton area for over 20 years.

Our story began in the winter of 2001, when our founder, Andrew Urquhart, realized there was a need for reliable and efficient snow removal services in the area. With just a small team and a single truck, he started clearing driveways and sidewalks for local residents.

As the years went by, our business grew and we expanded our services to include commercial properties as well. Today, we have a fleet of trucks and a team of dedicated professionals who work hard to ensure our clients' safety and satisfaction during the winter months.

At Urquhart's Snow Removal Ltd., we understand the importance of timely and efficient service. That's why we use the latest technology and equipment to get the job done quickly and effectively. We also prioritize safety, ensuring that our team members are trained and equipped to handle any situation.

We are proud of the work we do and the relationships we have built with our clients over the years. If you're looking for reliable and professional snow removal services in the Moncton area, look no further than Urquhart's Snow Removal Ltd. Contact us today to learn more about our services and how we can help you stay safe and warm this winter!

Our team of experienced technicians is fully licensed and insured, and we use only the latest equipment and technology to provide efficient and effective snow removal services. We also offer flexible contracts to suit your specific needs and budget.
